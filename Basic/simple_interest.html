Simple Interest

p=int(input("Enter the principle : "))
r=float(input("Enter the rate of interest: "))
t=int(input("Enter the number of years for which you want calculate simple interest: "))
si=(p*r*t)/100
print ("The Simple Interest is ", si)

OUTPUT :-

Enter the principle : 5000
Enter the rate of interest: 5
Enter the number of years for which you want to calculate simple interest: 2
The Simple Interest is 500.0
